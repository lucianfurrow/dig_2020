Habitat

The chicken lives in areas with dirt, grass, and high places to roost. Chickens came from Southeast Asia. They were around 10,000 - 8,000 years ago. The only wild chicken now is the Red Junglefowl. It lives in Southeast Asia.

Breeds

Nobody knows how many breeds of chickens there are in the world because nobody keeps track of them over the whole world. There are more than 500 fancy breeds in the world.

Ayam Cemani

It has no colors but black- everything on and in this chicken is black: its feathers, its eyes, its skin, its bones, even its heart.

Silkie

The first person who wrote about Silkies was Marco Polo. Nobody knows where they came from, but it might be ancient China. I have a silkie chick named Elsie.

Cream Legbar

The Cream Legbar was created at the genetical institute of Cambridge University in England. They look very different as babies than grownups. They are very rare in the United States. They lay blue eggs.
